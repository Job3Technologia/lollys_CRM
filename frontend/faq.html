Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from Lolly's

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! We are available on Uber Eats for fast and reliable delivery straight to your door. You can also place orders for collection via our website.

Is the meat Halal?

Absolutely. We are strictly 100% Halal certified. All our meats are sourced from certified suppliers and prepared in accordance with Halal standards.

How long does a collection order take?

Typically, orders are ready within 15-20 minutes. However, during peak lunch hours (12pm - 2pm), it might take up to 30 minutes. You can select your preferred pickup time during checkout.

Do you do catering for events?

Yes, we do! We offer platters and bulk orders for office parties, meetings, and private events. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to arrange catering.

What payment methods do you accept?

For online orders, you can pay via Card or Cash on Collection. We also accept all major credit/debit cards and Zapper in-store.

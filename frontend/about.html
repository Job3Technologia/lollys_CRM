Our Story

Bringing the authentic taste of Durban to your plate, one meal at a time.

The Soul of Durban

Founded in the heart of Durban Central, Lolly's Food Joint started with a simple mission: to serve real, unpretentious food that hits the spot every time.

From our legendary "Last Number Kota" to our rich, aromatic curries, every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and a whole lot of love. We believe in quality, community, and the power of a good meal to bring people together.
